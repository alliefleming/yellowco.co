Membership FAQ

Who is Yellow Co.?

We are a community of women passionate about living creatively and bravely as Agents of Good!

Our members stay connected with each other, growing and doing good, via our monthly membership subscription including digital resources, monthly chapter gatherings and our private Facebook group and Slack channel. We rally together once a year in Los Angeles for the Yellow Conference!

What is Yellow membership?

Yellow membership is a $33.00, monthly subscription connecting creative, entrepreneurial women that want to use their gifts, skills and talents to advance the common good. Our monthly subscription includes digital resources, monthly chapter gatherings and our private Facebook group and Slack channel. Attending the annual Yellow Conference is an additional fee.

How do I become a Yellow member?

Head over to members.yellowco.co/signup to create an account, enter your payment information and that's it! You'll instantly have access to our digital resources and online community. We'd love to have you as a member!

What do I get as a Yellow member?

You’ll have access to online resources and amazing community both online and in person (for those who are located near an in person chapter). Plus a monthly packet of Yellow goodness delivered to your doorstep. Together as creative, entrepreneurial women, we will be using the power of the collective to change the world. Your membership helps Yellow employ youths who are transitioning out of foster care, it helps fund sex-trafficking rescue operations, and it allows Yellow to support other life-changing initiatives taking place across the world!
